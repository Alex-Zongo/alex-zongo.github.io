I presented a poster at the **3rd Workshop on Multi-Agent Embodied Intelligent Systems Meet the Generative-AI Era (MEIS @ CVPR 2026)**, held during CVPR 2026 in Denver, Colorado.

The poster covered our work on **fine-tuning Large Language Models for cooperative tactical deconfliction of small Unmanned Aerial Systems** — a simulation-to-language data generation pipeline based on the BlueSky air traffic simulator, with parameter-efficient fine-tuning (LoRA and LoRA + GRPO) that improves decision accuracy and reduces near mid-air collisions.
