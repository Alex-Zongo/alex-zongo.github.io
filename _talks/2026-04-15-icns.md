I presented our work at the **26th Integrated Communications, Navigation and Surveillance (ICNS) Conference**, held April 14–16, 2026 at the Westin Washington Dulles Airport in Herndon, Virginia.

The talk covered our study of **eVTOL energy overhead under tactical conflict resolution** in high-density airspace: using a physics-based power model within a traffic simulation, we quantified the energy cost of Modified Voltage Potential (MVP) deconfliction and developed a machine-learning model that estimates reserve-energy needs at mission initiation.
