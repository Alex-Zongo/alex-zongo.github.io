Air Traffic Control, Autonomy, and the Physics of the Sky

Air Traffic Control is one of the most complex control systems ever built; balancing safety, efficiency, uncertainty, and human decision-making at a global scale. This blog explores modern and emerging approaches to airspace management, from classical ATC principles to physics-inspired models, reinforcement learning, and autonomous conflict resolution for the future of aviation.
